My Neighborhood

I currently live in Addison, TX. It's a relatively small city in Dallas just North of Interstate-635. Addison has residental neighborhoods as well as commercial spots. Some of the biggest tech companies are located in Addison like Bottle Rocket Apps and Nerium.

Restaurants

With around 15,000 residents and nearly 200 restaurants, Addison has the most restaurants per capita than any city in the US. Some of my favorite places to eat are:

  • Sherlock's Baker St. Pub and Grill
  • Vidorra
  • Neighborhood Services
  • Stirr
  • Yard House

Parks

Some of the best parks in Addison are Vitruvian Park and Addison Circle Park with the latter hosting an array of events throughout the year including Oktoberfest and Kaboom Town. Their are more parks to explore in Addison. Click here for the Addison, TX Parks website.
